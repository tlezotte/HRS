The Hendersonville Race Series groups together (for promotional purposes only) Hendersonville's four largest races: The Memorial Day Classic, the Hendersonville Half Marathon, the Indian Lake Loop and the Frostbike 5K, 10K. Each race is independently run and operated.


The purpose of the race series is two-fold: to promote health and fitness in our city, and to promote the City of Hendersonville. By encouraging participants to race, run, walk-run, or even walk the races, we hope to help our neighbors create a lifelong, lasting habit.


Runners that participate in multiple races this calendar year will receive:

  • three races finished, an HRS long sleeve tee shirt
  • all four races an HRS hoodie.
