The Hendersonville Race Series groups together (for promotional purposes only) Hendersonville's four largest races: The Memorial Day Classic, the Hendersonville Half Marathon, the Indian Lake Loop and the Frostbike 5K, 10K. Each race is independently run and operated.

The purpose of the race series is two-fold:

  • Promote health and fitness in our city
  • Promote the City of Hendersonville

By encouraging participants to race, run, walk-run, or even walk the races, we hope to help our neighbors create a lifelong, lasting habit.

Runners that participate in multiple races this calendar year will receive:

  • Three races participated, an HRS long sleeve tee shirt
  • All four races participated, an HRS hoodie

      FAQ

      Here are some Frequently Asked Questions

      What is the HRS?

      For promotional purposes, we have grouped Hendersonville's four largest races.

      Which races are included in the HRS?

      The Memorial Day Classic, the Hendersonville Half Marathon, the Indian Lake Loop and the Frostbite 5K/10K.

      How can I add my race to the series?

      At the moment we are not taking any additional races, but we expect that in the future we will make an announcement as to how your race may be added. Please refer back to this website for information.

      Do I have to register for the HRS?

      Yes. A minimal fee of $10 is required to be eligible to receive the gift. Please follow this link to do so:

      Is registering for the HRS the same thing as registering for the races?

      NO. You still have to register for each individual race. To do so, please visit their website or registration page.

      Can I purchase a tee shirt or a hoodie if I don't run the number of races required to receive either as a gift?

      NO. The tee shirt and hoodie are only for those who finish the required number of races.

      When may we expect our gifts?

      The gifts will be available about two to three weeks after the last race of the year.
